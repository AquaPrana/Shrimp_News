# Shrimp.News Project Requirements

## Project Overview

Shrimp.News is an India-focused shrimp media, education, aquaculture, market intelligence, and industry information platform.

Its primary mission is to increase domestic shrimp awareness and consumption in India while also covering shrimp prices, markets, farming, diseases, health, technology, and industry developments.

## Ask AquaPrana

Ask AquaPrana is an AI assistant focused on:

- Shrimp farming
- Water quality
- Shrimp diseases
- Shrimp nutrition
- Shrimp prices
- Shrimp markets
- Shrimp technology
- Shrimp.News articles

It should recommend relevant Shrimp.News articles when appropriate.

It should politely reject unrelated questions.

It must not provide unsafe chemical dosages, unsupported guarantees, or definite disease diagnoses without enough information.
